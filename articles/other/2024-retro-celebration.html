2024 Retro Celebration Events at NAIC

By: PTCG Legends | @PTCG_Legends | April 27, 2024

ios_share Share Page:   Tweet   

Let's Celebrate Retro!

Historically, at least in recent years, there have been exciting retro events at each NAIC - having drawn countless players together to celebrate the greatest TCG of all time. Each one run by amazing individuals that sought to spread the joy of retro with others, each more memorable than the last. This year however, with no previous organizers set to return, we're taking this opportunity to fill in the gap. And who better than THE LARGEST retro website in the world!

At this year's North American International Championships, PTCG Legends will be hosting two retro events in the New Orleans Convention Center! Play for cool prizes and mark your place in history - as all players and decklists will be archived here on PTCG Legends! But whether you want to compete (free to play) or spectate, we welcome all players from around the world to come hang out with us!

Event details below - follow us on twitter X for updates. @PTCG_Legends

  • 2014 "10-Year Anniversary" Event


2010 World's Retro Event


Let's celebrate one of the greatest formats of all time together! Not a single sane-person dislikes 2010, so its a natural choice for an event format!

When: Thursday, June 6

Time: TBD (Currently set at 12pm on Limitless, likely to change.)

Where: New Orleans Convention Center

The exact spot is TBD. We already have a couple locations within the venue in mind and have confirmed with the convention center staff areas we can use, so fear not, we'll make it happen!

Entry: FREE

Registration: Link

Decklists required - must register on Limitless

Proxies: Printed proxies and cards of any language ARE allowed.

World Championship cards are also allowed, as long as sleeves are not see-through if mixed with real or Japanese cards. If using printed proxies, please have them printed in color and placed on top of a regular Pok√©mon card within the sleeve. Black and white proxies are incredibly hard to see for many players, so please avoid if possible.

Format: 2010 World's (Diamond Pearl - HGSS Unleashed)

Structure: Best of 1 Swiss - 35 minute rounds
