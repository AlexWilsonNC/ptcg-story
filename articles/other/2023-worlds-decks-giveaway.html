What's inside these World Championship decks?


Each deck contains:

  • 60-card World Championships Deck
  • 2023 World Championships Booklet
  • Special 2023 World Championships Pin
  • 2023 World Championships Coin
  • 2023 World Championships Deck Box (cardboard)
  • A paper Playmat and Poster
  • A code card for Pokémon Trading Card Game Live


(The cards printed in these decks are not allowed in sanctioned tournament play.)

What Decks were Printed?


Vance Kelley - 1st Place 2023 World Championships (Masters)


Tord Reklev - 2nd Place 2023 World Championships (Masters)


Gabriel Fernandez - 1st Place 2023 World Championships (Seniors)
