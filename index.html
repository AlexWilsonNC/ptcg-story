Welcome to PTCG Legends


Whether you're a retro deck builder, a veteran player reminiscing on the past, or wanting to take a look into the World Championships with the dream of becoming the next World Champion, this site is catered to all interested in the competitve side of the Pokemon TCG.


What you can Find Here


• Every World Championships: Each event's info, final results data, galleries of images from each event, all available streams and event coverage from each event, and stories & social media from players and staff from said events!


• International Championships and older National events can be found under "Major Events", each event having their own dedicated section just like Worlds mentioned above.


• A plathora of decks from each year/era can be found in the top nav. Be warned, try not to get lost, there's a lot of decks...


• Under the "Other" tab in the top nav, you can find some really neat pages, including older format rules, a history of banned cards, resources for anyone interested in something more that may not be found here, and a section to remember our friends and family members from the Pokemon community that we lost too soon.


• If you'd like to ever submit a decklist that you feel is missing or wrong, report false info, dedicate a page to someone close that's passed away, or simply provide insight on what we can change; please use the contact link in the footer below.



Message from the Creator


~ Alex Wilson


" I've been playing the Pokemon Trading Card Game since 2010, but I've been enveloped by Pokemon since I was in Kindergarten, which is for as long as I can remember... While I'm more so known for being a top competitor in the XY - Sun & Moon era, I'm an avid "retro" deck collector, and love reminiscing on the past of my favorite hobby. Often times, I find myself scrolling through old forums looking for decks that I may have forgotten about, or re-watching Worlds streams late at night.


While there's been plenty of sites out there that have provided amazing information, they all seemed to stick to one niche, only decklists, just results, players stats.... Personally, I was tired of having to remember which website contained whichever desired info that I was searching for, thus, I took things into my own hands and created this massive site that has every possible grain of information about historic, past events, one could ever hope for.


I recently became a web developer, so this was an enjoyable side project while practing outside of my full-time development job. This is what I've always envisioned in a retro TCG website, so I hope you all enjoy the content that can be found here and I hope to meet you at events throughout the future. "
