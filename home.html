Welcome to PTCG Legends

Whether you're a retro deck builder, a veteran player reminiscing on the past, or wanting to take a look back at the World Championships with the dream of becoming the next, this site is catered to all interested in the competitve side of the Pokémon TCG.

Decks from Every Era

Want to look back at your favorite decks? More than 1,000 decks from throughout the years can be found here. Warning: there's a lot of them, try not to get lost...

World Championships

Each event's info, final results data, event image galleries, all available streams and event coverage from each event, plus stories & social media posts from players and staff, all found here!

Message from the Creator

~ Alex Wilson

"I've been playing the Pokémon Trading Card Game since 2010, but I've been enveloped by Pokémon since I was in Kindergarten in 1999. While I'm more-so known for being a top competitor in the XY - Sun & Moon era, I'm an avid "retro" deck collector, and love reminiscing on the past of what will forever be my favorite hobby. Often times, I find myself scrolling through old forums looking for decks that I may have forgotten about, or re-watching Worlds streams late at night.


While there's been plenty of sites out there that have provided amazing information, they all seemed to stick to one niche, only decklists from select years, just results, player's stats... Personally, I was tired of having to remember which website contained whichever desired info that I was searching for, thus, I took things into my own hands and created this massive site that has every possible grain of information about historic, past events, one could ever hope for!"
