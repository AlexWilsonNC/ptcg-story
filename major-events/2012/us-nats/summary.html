June 30 - July 1, 2012

Indianapolis, Indiana

2012 US National Championships

HeartGold & SoulSilver - BW: Dark Explorers

Indiana Convention Center

100 South Capitol Ave

• The 2012 US National Championships was held in Indianapolis. In order to compete, Master division players must have earned a minimum of 10 Play Points from throughout the season. Whereas, Seniors and Juniors needed a minimum of 5 Play Points. Registration was gaurateed to the first 640 players that showed up, though 1,005 players were permitted into the event.

• Friday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which the Top 128 players moved on to a brutal single-elimination Top Cut on Saturday, saving Sunday for the remaining Top 16 players.

Prizing:

• 1st Place

$5,000

• 2nd Place

$3,000

• Top 4

$1,500

(Top 2 finishers received 2 Booster Boxes.)

(Top 4 - Top 16 finishers received 1 Booster Box each.)

(Top 32 finishers received 18 Booster Packs.)
