Nov 15 - 17, 2019

São Paulo, Brazil

2020 Latin American International Championships

SM: Ultra Prism - SM: Cosmic Eclipse

Centro de Eventos PRO MAGNO

513 – Jardim das Laranjeiras

• The 2020 Latin American International Championships returned to São Paulo for a forth season in a row. It was a three-day event where anyone was welcome to play.

• Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 726 players competed in Day 1. All players with 19 match points moved on to Day 2. (88 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 5 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
