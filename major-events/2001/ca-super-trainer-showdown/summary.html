Dec 1 - 2, 2001

San Diego, California US

2001 CA Super Trainer Showdown

Team Rocket - Neo Revelation

San Diego Convention Center

111 Harbor Dr

• The 2001 California (West Coast) Super Trainer Showdown was the last ever STS event in North America.

• Super Trainer Showdowns were two-day events, in which there were two different tournaments held on both Saturday and Sunday. (Fun Fact: Though each day's events were equally prestigious, many player's considered Day 2's event as more meta-defining, given that players were able to imporove their lists after the first day's event.)

• This was the only STS in which there were four age divisions. The 11-to-12 & 13-to-14 age divisions were split due largely to the overwhelming numbers in the 11-to-14 age group in the previous Super Trainer Showdown in New Jersey.
