July 17, 2004

Rockville, Maryland US

2004 East Coast Stadium Challenge

Expedition - EX Hidden Legends

Quality Suites Hotel

• The 2004 East Coast Stadium Challenge was the last ever Stadium Challenge (alongside the WCSC held on the same weekend), eventually getting replaced by the more modern Regional Championships.

• Approximately 132 players attended this event, with the Top 4 players earning an invite to the 2004 World Championships. (With the two finalists earning a travel award as well.)

Prizing:

(Top 4 players recieved an invitation to 2004 Worlds.)

(1st Place received 72 Booster Packs.)

(2nd Place received 44 Booster Packs.)

(Top 4 finishers received 36 Booster Packs.)

(Top 8 finishers received 18 Booster Packs.)

(Top 12 finishers received 9 Booster Packs.)

(Top 16 finishers received 5 Booster Packs.)
