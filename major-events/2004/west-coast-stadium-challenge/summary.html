July 17, 2004

Berkeley, California US

2004 West Coast Stadium Challenge

Expedition - EX Hidden Legends

University of California, Berkeley

UC Berkeley

• The 2004 West Coast Stadium Challenge was the last ever Stadium Challenge in North America (alongside the ECSC held on the same weekend), eventually getting replaced by the more modern Regional Championships.

• Approximately 115 players attended this event, with the Top 4 players earning an invite to the 2004 World Championships. (With the two finalists earning a travel award as well.)

Prizing:

(Top 4 players recieved an invitation to 2004 Worlds.)

(1st Place received 72 Booster Packs.)

(2nd Place received 44 Booster Packs.)

(Top 4 finishers received 36 Booster Packs.)

(Top 8 finishers received 18 Booster Packs.)

(Top 12 finishers received 9 Booster Packs.)

(Top 16 finishers received 5 Booster Packs.)
