July 5 - 7, 2013

Indianapolis, Indiana

2013 US National Championships

Black & White - BW: Plasma Freeze

Indiana Convention Center

100 South Capitol Ave

• The 2013 US National Championships was held in Indianapolis. In order to compete, Master division players must have earned a minimum of 15 Play Points from throughout the season. Whereas, Seniors and Juniors needed a minimum of 5 Play Points.

• Friday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 927 players competed in Day 1, seperated into two different pods. (NOTE: There was a cap in place of 972 Master spots.) The Top 128 players moved on to a brutal single-elimination Top Cut on Saturday (75 min rounds), saving Sunday for the remaining Top 16 players.

• This was the only US Nationals event to ever host a "Last-Chance" Tournament on Sunday, for players to earn Championship Points towards a Worlds invite one last time. This event was Best of One and single-elimination, until 16 players were left standing. (Pictured below)
