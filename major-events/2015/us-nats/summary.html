July 3 - 5, 2015

Indianapolis, Indiana

2015 US National Championships

BW: Boundaries Crossed - XY: Roaring Skies

Indiana Convention Center

100 South Capitol Ave

• The 2015 US National Championships was held in Indianapolis for the final time. In order to compete, Master division players must have earned a minimum of 10 Play Points from throughout the season. Whereas, Seniors and Juniors needed a minimum of 5 Play Points.

• Friday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 911 players competed in Day 1, seperated into two different pods. (NOTE: There was a cap in place of 924 Master spots) The Top 32 players from each pod moved on to a combined Day 2 of 64 players. Day 2 consisted of 6 additional round of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.

Prizing:

• 1st Place

$5,000

• 2nd Place

$2,500

• Top 4

$1,500

(Top 2 finishers received 12 Booster Boxes.)

(Top 8 finishers received 6 Booster Boxes.)

(Top 16 finishers received 4 Booster Boxes.)

(Top 32 finishers received 2 Booster Boxes.)

(Top 64 finishers received 1 Booster Box each.)

(Top 128 finishers received 18 Booster Packs.)
