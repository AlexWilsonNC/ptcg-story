June 27 - 28, 2009

St. Louis, Missouri

2009 US National Championships

Diamond & Pearl - DP: Rising Rivals

America's Center Convention Complex

701 Convention Plaza

• The 2009 US National Championships was held in St. Louis. This was also the last Nationals that was a two-day event.

• Saturday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 679 players competed in Day 1. The Top 128 players moved on to a brutal single-elimination Top Cut on Sunday.

Prizing:

• 1st Place

$5,000

• 2nd Place

$3,000

• Top 4

$1,500

(All cash prizing was awarded via "scholarships".)

(All Top 8 players recieved an invitation to 2009 Worlds.)

(Top 2 finishers received 72 Booster Packs.)

(Top 16 finishers received 36 Booster Packs.)
