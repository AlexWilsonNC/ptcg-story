June 28 - 29, 2008

Columbus, Ohio

2008 US National Championships

EX Holon Phantoms - DP: Majestic Dawn

Greater Columbus Convention Center

400 N High St

• The 2008 US National Championships was held in Columbus for the last time ever.

• Saturday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 536 players competed in Day 1.

Prizing:

• 1st Place

$5,000

• 2nd Place

$3,000

• Top 4

$1,500

(All cash prizing was awarded via "scholarships".)

(All Top 8 players recieved an invitation to 2008 Worlds.)

(Top 2 finishers received 72 Booster Packs.)

(Top 16 finishers received 36 Booster Packs.)
