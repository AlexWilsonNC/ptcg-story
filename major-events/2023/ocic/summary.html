Feb 17 - 19, 2023

Melbourne, Australia

2023 Oceania International Championships

Sword & Shield - Crown Zenith

Melbourne Convention & Exhibition Centre

1 Convention Centre Pl

• The 2023 Oceania International Championships returned to Melbourne for a third season in a row, but forth time ever. It was a three-day event where anyone was welcome to play. (NOTE: The event initially capped out at 420 players, though 562 spots total were later made available.)

• Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 528 players competed in Day 1. All players with 19 match points moved on to Day 2. (60 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 5 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
