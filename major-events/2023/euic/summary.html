April 14 - 16, 2023

London, United Kingdom

2023 European International Championships

Battle Styles - Scarlet & Violet

ExCeL London

Royal Victoria Dock, One Western Gateway

• The 2023 European International Championships returned to London for the first time since 2018, making this the third EUIC held in London. It was a three-day event where anyone was welcome to play. (NOTE: The event initially capped out at ___ players, though ___ spots total were later made available.)

• Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which ___ players competed in Day 1. All players with 19 match points moved on to Day 2. (___ players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of ___ additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.

• There was a pop-up Pokémon Center for the first ever outside of NAIC or Worlds. But it was only open Friday - Sunday and required an online reservation to enter.

• This was the first event by Play! Pokémon utilizing the first-ever "block" format rotation. Cards were no longer legal by the set they're in, but rather, legal based on a letter found on the bottom left corner of a card, known as their regulation mark.
