June 30 - July 2, 2023

Columbus, Ohio

2023 North American International Championships

Battle Styles - Paldea Evolved

Greater Columbus Convention Center

400 N High St

• The 2023 North American International Championships returned to Columbus for a fifth year in a row. It was a three-day event where anyone was welcome to play. (NOTE: All three waves of this event's online registration capped out within seconds, followed by a forth limited wave letting a few more players into the event just one week prior.)

• Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 1897 players competed in Day 1. All players with 19 match points or more moved on to Day 2. (193 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 6 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.

• For the first time ever, during the final trophy ceremony, Anna Prosser (the host) made a surprise announcement showing off some early details on a few Regional & Special Event locations to look forward to in the 2024 season.
