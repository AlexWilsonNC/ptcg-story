July 6 - 8, 2018

Columbus, Ohio

2018 North American International Championships

XY: BREAKthrough - SM: Forbidden Light

Greater Columbus Convention Center

400 N High St

• The 2018 North American International Championships was held in Columbus for the first time. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 1,534 players were seperated into two different pods. The Top 32 players from each pod moved on to a combined Day 2. (64 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 6 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
