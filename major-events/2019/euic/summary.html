Apr 26 - 28, 2019

Berlin, Germany

2019 European International Championships

Sun & Moon - SM: Team Up

Estrel Berlin Congress Center

Sonnenallee 225, Berlin

• The 2019 European International Championships was held in Germany for the first time. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 819 players competed in Day 1. All players with 19 match points moved on to Day 2. (88 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 6 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
