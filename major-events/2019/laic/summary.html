Nov 16 - 18, 2018

São Paulo, Brazil

2019 Latin America International Championships

Sun & Moon - SM: Lost Thunder

Centro de Eventos PRO MAGNO

513 - Jardim das Laranjeiras

• The 2019 Latin America International Championships was held in São Paulo for a third season in a row. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 630 players competed in Day 1. Day 2 consisted of 5 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
