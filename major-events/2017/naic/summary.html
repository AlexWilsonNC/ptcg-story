June 30 - July 2, 2017

Indianapolis, Indiana

2017 North American International Championships

XY: Primal Clash - SM: Guardians Rising

Indiana Convention Center

100 South Capitol Ave

• The 2017 North American International Championships was held in Indianapolis for the only time. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 1,356 players were seperated into two different pods. The Top 32 players from each pod moved on to a combined Day 2. (64 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 6 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
