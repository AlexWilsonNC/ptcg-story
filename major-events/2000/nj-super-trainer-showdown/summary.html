November 8, 2000

Secaucus, New Jersey US

2000 NJ Super Trainer Showdown

(Prop 15/3) Base Set - Gym Challenge

Meadowlands Exposition Center

355 Plaza Dr

• The 2000 New Jersey (East Coast) Super Trainer Showdown was the second STS event, with over 700 total competitors across all age divisions.

• Players competed in Eight best-of-one Rounds, with a Top Cut of 8.

• This was the only major event that utilized the format titled, “15/3”. Players were to construct 60-card decks using only fifteen “Trainer” cards and only three of any one card (instead of the usual four), not counting Basic Energy Cards. This forced players to run more Pokemon and Energy Cards than usual and forced variety in the cards used, due to the 3-max stipulation.
