PTCGO

April - July, 2021

Players Cup IV

SM: Team Up - SSH: Chilling Reign

• The 2021 Players Cup IV tournament was the last of the world-wide Players Cup series that took place from 2020-2021 while sanctioned events were not occuring. It was an online event that was played using PTCGO.

• Though this event's Grand Finals format was played using Team Up - Chilling Reign. The Qualifier rounds, explained below, were played using Team Up - Battle Styles.

• Everyone recieved 50 "keys" (or tokens) in which they could use to compete in fifty 8-man-bracket tournaments on PTCGO. Each of these brackets were Bo1 single-elimination. The higher one placed in the brackets, the more points were earned. After a set period of time, the Top X players via points were eligible to compete in their Region's Qualifiers.

• For North America, the Top 256 players moved on into their Region's Qualifiers, and the same for Europe and Latin America. However, only the Top 128 players from Oceania moved on to their Qualifiers. Each region's Qualifier event was a double-elimination tournament.

• The Top 4 players from each Region's Qualifier earned their spot into the Top 16 to compete in the Grand Finals!
