Aug 19 - 21, 2005

San Diego, California

2005 World Championships

EX Ruby & Sapphire - EX Emerald

Town & Country San Diego

500 Hotel Cir N

• The 2005 Pokémon World Championships took place in San Diego for the first, of eventually, three times, and was the second Worlds officially hosted by Pokémon.

• Approximately 434 total players attended Worlds this year.

• A Last Chance Qualifier (the Grinder) was held on Friday, before the main event. The Top 8 players from the Grinder earned a spot to compete in the World Championships the next day. (252 players competed in the Grinder)

Prizing:

~ Prized out as scholarships.

• 1st Place

$7,500

• 2nd Place

$5,000

• Top 4

$3,500

• Top 8

$1,500

(The Top 4 finishers received an automatic invitation to the 2006 World Championships.)
