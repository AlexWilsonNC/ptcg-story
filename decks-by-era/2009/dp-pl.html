DuskZong

Source: @Pkmn_RubyRetro

Don't let the paragraph of text on Dusknoir Lv.X intimidate you, because this is a spread deck you want to try out! Once Dusknoir is activated as a Stadium, and paired with Bronzong, damage counters swarm the opponent's board seemingly non-stop. Cresselia aids in reaching KOs quicker, Azelf helps in allowing the deck to function in a format with Tyranitar, and Omastar cleans up games by decimating every opponent's Pokémon in play.
