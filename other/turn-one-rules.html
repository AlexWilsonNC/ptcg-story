Rules by Era

Highlighted text, points out which rules changed from the previous era.


• Players flip coin AFTER setup - winner of flip MUST go first.

• For each mulligan, opponent may draw up to 2 cards per mulligan, as they occur.

(if a player's opening hand contains only a Fossil(s) card, they may choose to mulligan)

• Player going First:

~ player draws a card to start their turn

~ player has no card restrictions

~ player CAN attack

• Players may retreat as many times per turn as they choose.

• If player's Pokémon is confused & wants to retreat: pay retreat cost, flip a coin.

~ If Heads:

• retreat like normal

~ If Tails:

• retreat fails, leaving Pokémon confused and remaining Active

• confused Pokémon does 20 damage to itself (weakness & resistance applies)

• confused Pokémon can NOT attempt to retreat again this turn

• Players CAN play multiple Stadium cards per turn.

(Including Stadiums of the same name)

("Stadiums" are simply Trainers)

• Weakness x2

• Resistance -30

• Poison = 1 damage counter between turns

• Confusion = 20 dmg (weakness & resistance applies)

• Burn = coin flip between turns

~ if heads = no damage, but Pokémon remains burned

~ if tails = 2 damage counters and Pokémon remains burned
