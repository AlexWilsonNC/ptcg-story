Aug 9 - 11

Vancouver, B.C. Canada

2013 World Championships

BW (Base Set) - BW: Plasma Freeze

Vancouver Convention Center

1055 Canada Place

• The 2013 Pokemon World Championships took place outside of the US for its first time ever. It was a two day event, (3 counting the LCQ), where anyone who met the Championship Point threshold was invited to play in Day 1 on Saturday. Of which consisted of 9 Rounds, directly followed by a Top Cut of 16. 209 players competed in the main event.

• Like each Worlds before it, this year included a Last Chance Qualifier. Jason Klaczynski made history in this year by not only winning his 3rd World Championship, but fought his way through the 2013 Last Chance Qualifier just to compete in Worlds, the first ever Masters division player to do so!

Championship Point Threshold

~ Players that won their countries' National Championships earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.

~ Players that went undefeated in the LCQ earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.
