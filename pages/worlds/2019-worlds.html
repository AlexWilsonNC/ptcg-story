2019 World Championships

Format: Ultra Prism - Unified Minds

The 2019 Pokemon World Championships returned to Washington D.C. for the second time in history. It was a three day event, where anyone who met the Championship Point threshold was invited to play in Day 1 on Friday. Which consisted of 8 Rounds, anyone with a record of 5-0-3, or 6 wins, moved on to Day 2.

Day 2 (Saturday) consisted of the above players that made it through a rough Day 1, as well as the Top X players from throughout the season, seperated by region, found below. Day 2 was also 8 Rounds long, but resulting in a Top 8 Cut.
