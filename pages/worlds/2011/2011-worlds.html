Aug 12 - 14

San Diego, California

2011 World Championships

HeartGold & SoulSilver - Black & White

Hilton San Diego Bayfront

1 Park Blvd, San Diego CA

• The 2011 Pokemon World Championships took place in San Diego for the third and final time. It was a two day event, (3 counting the LCQ), where anyone who met the Championship Point threshold was invited to play in Day 1 on Saturday. Of which consisted of 7 Rounds, directly followed by a Top Cut of 16. 122 players competed in the main event.

• Like each Worlds before it, this year included a Last Chance Qualifier. LCQ competitors played 6 rounds on Friday, of which only players with an undefeated record of 6-0 earned a Last Chance Invite.

• On July 1st of 2011, for the first time in TCG history, Play! Pokemon announced an emergency mid-season rotation due to Sableye Donk, making this Worlds the first ever to use a newly "modified" format. (7 sets were cut from format, the largest rotation ever at the time!)

Championship Point Threshold

~ Players that won their countries' National Championships earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.

~ Players that went undefeated in the LCQ earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.
