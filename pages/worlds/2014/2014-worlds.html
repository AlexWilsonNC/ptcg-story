Aug 15 - 17

Washington D.C. USA

2014 World Championships

BW: Next Destinies - XY: Flashfire

Walter E. Washington Convention Center

801 Mount Vernon Place NW

• The 2014 Pokemon World Championships took place in Washington D.C. for the first time ever. It was a two day event, (3 counting the LCQ), where anyone who met the Championship Point threshold was invited to play in Day 1 on Saturday. Of which consisted of 7 Rounds, directly followed by a Top Cut of 8. 231 players competed in the main event.

• This event was the last Worlds that included a Last Chance Qualifier. Each World Championships from 2007 - 2014 had an LCQ. The 2014 World's LCQ was played out on Friday and consisted of 7 rounds. Players with an undefeated record of 7-0 earned an "invitation" to compete in the World Championships the next day.

Championship Point Threshold

~ Players that won their countries' National Championships earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.

~ Players that went undefeated in the LCQ earned an automatic Invitation to Worlds.
