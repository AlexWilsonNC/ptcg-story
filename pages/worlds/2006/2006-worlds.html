Anaheim, California

2006 World Championships

EX Hidden Legends - EX Holon Phantoms

Hilton Anaheim

777 Convention Way

• The 2006 Pokémon World Championships took place in Anaheim for the first time and was the third Worlds officially hosted by Pokémon. It was a two day event, of which Saturday consisted of 8 Rounds, followed by a single-elimination Top Cut of 32 on Sunday.

• This was the final year of premier events before divisions were reworked and named what we're familiar with today, "Juniors, Seniors, & Masters."
