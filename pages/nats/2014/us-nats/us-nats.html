July 4 - 6, 2014

Indianapolis, Indiana

2014 US National Championships

BW: Next Destinies - XY: Flashfire

Indiana Convention Center

100 South Capitol Ave

• The 2014 US National Championships was held in Indianapolis. In order to compete, Master division players must have earned a minimum of 10 Play Points from throughout the season. Whereas Seniors and Juniors needed a minimum of 5 Play Points.

• Friday consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 889 players competed in Day 1, seperated into two different pods. (NOTE: There was a cap in place of 972 Master spots) The Top 32 players from each pod moved on to a combined Day 2 of 64 players. Day 2 consisted of 6 additional round of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 16. Top 16 through Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
