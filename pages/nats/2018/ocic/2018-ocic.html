Feb 9 - 11, 2018

Sydney, Australia

2018 Oceania International Championships

XY: BREAKthrough - SM: Crimson Invasion

Sydney Olympic Park

8 Australia Avenue

• The 2018 Oceania International Championships was held in Sydney for the first time. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 333 players competed in Day 1. Day 2 consisted of 5 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.

• This event shared the same format as the European International Championships, just a few months prior.
