Feb 15 - 17, 2019

Melbourne, Australia

2019 Oceania International Championships

Sun & Moon - SM: Team Up

Melbourne Convention & Exhibition Centre

1 Convention Centre Pl

• The 2019 Oceania International Championships was held in Melbourne for a second time. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Bo3), in which 338 players competed in Day 1. Day 2 consisted of 5 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.
