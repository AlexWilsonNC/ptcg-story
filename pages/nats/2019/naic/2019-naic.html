June 21 - 23, 2019

Columbus, Ohio

2019 North American International Championships

Sun & Moon - SM: Unbroken Bonds

Greater Columbus Convention Center

400 N High St

• The 2019 North American International Championships returned to Columbus for a second year in a row. Day 1 consisted of 9 rounds (Best of 3), in which 1,122 players were seperated into two different pods. All players with 19 match points from each pod moved on to a combined Day 2. (112 players competed in Day 2) Day 2 consisted of 6 additional rounds of Swiss, resulting in a cut of Top 8. Top 8 & Top 4 matches were played out that night, saving each division's finals for Championship Sunday.

• This event featured the first-ever Pokémon Center at a North American event that was not a World Championship.
