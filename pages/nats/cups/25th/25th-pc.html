2021 Players Cup Invitational

SM: Team Up - SSH: Chilling Reign

Aug 13 - 15, 2021

PTCGO

• The 2021 Players Cup Invitational tournament was the finale, and 25th Pokemon Anniversary celebration, to the Players Cup series that took place from 2020-2021 during the peak of covid. It was an online event that was played using PTCGO. The four previous Players Cup Champions, and four World Champions, were invited to play in this exclusive event.

• The 8 invitees competed in a double elimination bracket, where the eventual Champion squared off in a final "Boss Battle" against the 2004 Pokemon World Champion, Tsuguyoshi Yamato.
