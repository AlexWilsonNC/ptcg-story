June 25 - 27

Indianapolis, Indiana

2010 US National Championships

Diamond & Pearl - HGSS: Unleashed

Indiana Convention Center

100 S Capitol Ave

• The 2010 US National Championships was held in Indianapolis for the first time ever. This was also the first Nationals that was a three day event where anyone was welcome to play, additionally event registration was free. Registration was gaurateed to the first 640 players that showed up, though more than 850 players were permitted into the event.

• Friday & Saturday consisted of 9 rounds (Bo3), in which the Top 128 players moved on to a brutal single-elimination top cut to decide the National Champion.

• Top 4
