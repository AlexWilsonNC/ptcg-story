Jungle expansion released.

First ever official Pokemon Trading Card Game Tournament takes place in Chiba, Japan during the 6th "Next Generation World Hobby Fair."

"Leaders Stadium" preconstructed decks (Brock & Misty) released. These decks built up to the release of the expansion, Gym Heroes. (Other Leader's decks released at a later time.)
