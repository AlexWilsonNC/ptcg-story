Arcanine Magmar

Source: Guy Bennett

While this deck wasn't by any means a competitive deck that could take on the likes of Haymaker or Rain Dance, we HAD to include a list with good ol' Charizard. Funny enough, this was an incredibly strong deck on the Gameboy Color game, "Pokémon Trading Card Game". Magmar and Arcanine are this deck's main focus, with Charizard as a late-game attacker.
